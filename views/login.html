Synopsis

A wiki-like program that lets you write articles in commonmark style markdown. 

**Markdown** is the formatting language that is frequently used in a lot of programs, such as the popular *Discord*. It looks exactly like this little blurb.

# Features

- Making lists
- Headers
- Bolding and Italics

Description

A note taking service for Roleplayers. It uses markdown-it behind the scenes. You will be able to make use of vanity urls and visibility controls for your articles.

This service will continue adding features for roleplayers as it ages. It takes inspiration from the Info and Finger systems from the MUSH Roleplaying service, which allowed users to share documentation about their characters easily.

Current Features

  • Write articles in markdown
  • Control whether an article is private, unlisted, or publicly listed
  • Account page that shows all your public notes
  • Titling and summarizing your articles
  • Exporting all your notes in markdown and html
